    Testimonials

    Features

    Communities around your school

    Share homework, projects and other school-related information with parents from your child's school. Multiple kids? No problem. One school community for each child, with organized and categorized information for each school/child.

    Communities outside the school

    Parent collaboration does not stop at school. You can create your own private groups or micro-communities for activities outside school too. Now your daughter's Girls Scout group and your son's Soccer group, along with your school communities, can all live happily together in one place.

    Safety - peace of mind

    Safety of kids and families is our highest priority. We do not permit anybody and everybody to join the community. Only those parents whose profiles and information are verified are allowed to participate in the community.

    Recommendations are just a click away

    Looking for a recommendation for an after-school activity for your child or for carpool help? Browse parent recommendations for various services or just reach out to your community and ask for one.

    Helpful updates from your local community

    Put an end to newsletter subscriptions and website bookmarks. Now you can find information on local family events, volunteering opportunities and other helpful updates, all in one place.
